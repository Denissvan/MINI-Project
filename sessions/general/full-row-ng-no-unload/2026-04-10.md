## Observed Symptom

- One PC may return a full row of NG results.
- Later the station does not unload, or the row is reread/retested, or results appear to return to pending state.
- Related field phenomena also included pause/resume causing stale result reuse and unload not occurring after exceptions.

## Current Hypothesis

- This demand actually contains two tightly related root-cause chains:
  1. DLL-side stale-result reread / fake-start behavior.
  2. C# station state not being consumed into unload in time after exceptions or partial workflow interruption.
- A key DLL issue is `StartTestFlow Skip -> RES_OK`, which can let old result-area data be read again by `WaitTestResult`.
- Another DLL issue is final incomplete packets such as `sta=0,n=0` reaching C#.
- C# side can also keep workstation unload-ready state or old results alive after updownload exceptions or pause/resume interruption.
